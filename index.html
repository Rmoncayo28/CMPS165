California's Quality of Life

How does income correlate to one's ability to receive a college education and healthcare?
Our visualisation shows that populations with high poverty levels are less likely to go college and receive adequate health coverage.
In contrast, wealthy areas enjoy high college enrollment rates and adequate healthcare.
