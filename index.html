California's Quality of Life

There is a stark divide in the quality of life of Californians in which you can gauge one's quality of life based on which region of California they live in.
As you move inland, poverty levels increase in comparison to areas along the coast such as the Bay Area and Los Angeles.
As poverty rises, college enrollment and health coverage rates trend downward, with the lowest percentages found along the central valley and eastern border.
